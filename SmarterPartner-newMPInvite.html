FAQs

Why do I need to register Zucora Complete and Zucora Standard plans before I can set up my Smarter Partner account?

It’s critical that all Zucora Complete and Zucora Standard plans are registered because effective June 30, 2020, these plan types will no longer be available for sale. Failure to register them will mean that the customer does not have coverage. All registered plans will be visible to you on the Smarter Partner system, however; you will not be able to edit or add information to these plan types.

Why is banking information required?

Your banking information must be provided in order for ZucoraHome to deliver your commissions for the sale and ongoing monthly payments of the Smarter Living Plan.

Is adding my financial information required?

The Smarter Partner Program goal is to make business operations simple so you can spend more time with your sales associates and customers. Adding your financial information when you set up your Profile in the Smarter Partner system allows ZucoraHome to deliver payments directly to you and creates an easy-to-use process for purchasing plans and products. The program also creates opportunities for you such as free shipping and earning rewards - both of which lead to more savings for you.

Are there payment options?

Yes, the Smarter Partner system lets you customize your payment preferences. For example, you may select pre-authorized debit for purchasing furniture care kits or PureCare products, or they can be charged to your credit card, or if applicable, charged to your Buying Group account.

I am a member of a Buying Group. Does this change what financial information is required when I’m setting up my account?

Banking information is required, but due to your arrangement with a Buying Group, your credit card details are only required if you choose to purchase furniture care kits and PureCare products with it rather than through your account with the Buying Group.
